Satori Salvation

Satori Salvation, a brand new luxury CBD brand, embarked on a packaging design that would amplify market presence while adhering to its core theme of enlightenment. The new packaging utilized high-end jewel tones and featured glossy rose gold accents, which elevated the product's visual appeal and underscored its premium positioning. The logo’s signature woven lines, emblematic of the path to enlightenment, were artistically expanded into wavy, intertwined patterns at each label's base. This sophisticated design not only attracted the luxury consumer but also effectively communicated the brand’s commitment to quality and the deeper philosophical ethos of enlightenment.

Happy Camper

Happy Camper, committed to environmental sustainability, developed packaging for its "Fire Sticks" pre-roll line that was both compostable and recyclable, aligning with Washington state’s ecological standards. The packaging for Sativa (Sunrise), Hybrid (Noon), and Indica (Sunset) varieties featured distinct, thematic artwork that not only differentiated the products but also unified the brand’s visual identity across its product range. This approach reinforced Happy Camper’s dedication to sustainability and provided consumers with an eco-friendly choice that did not compromise on design or functionality.

New England Hemp Farm

New England Hemp Farm sought to infuse its packaging with elements that reflected its rich local heritage and traditional values. The packaging design incorporated traditional sketch artwork of a hemp plant, adding texture and a handcrafted feel that resonated with the brand’s artisanal approach. This thoughtful design strategy highlighted the brand's commitment to authenticity and quality, enhancing its appeal in a competitive market where provenance and purity are highly valued.

Red Dirt

Red Dirt, known for its naturally sungrown cannabis, required packaging that communicated its commitment to traditional cultivation methods. The design incorporated vintage aesthetics and elements that highlighted the natural growing conditions, such as sunburst motifs and earthy colors, creating a package that spoke to the authenticity and quality of the product. This design not only attracted consumers looking for organically grown cannabis but also effectively differentiated Red Dirt in the marketplace as a brand with deep roots and genuine cultivation practices.

Bison & Rose

Bison & Rose introduced a new line of Metered Dose Inhalers with a packaging design that needed to clearly communicate the product's varying intensity levels while maintaining a luxurious feel appropriate for its high-end market. The packaging for the Balanced and Intense SKUs utilized distinct color schemes and sophisticated artwork to denote the differences in product strength, enhancing the consumer's experience by simplifying choice and reinforcing the brand’s commitment to quality and clarity.

Laughing Buddha

Laughing Buddha extended the distinctive street art style of its brand identity into the packaging design for its custom glass jars. The jars featured labels with a detailed street map of Seattle, overlaid with gold foil that added a level of sophistication and urban chic to the product. This design effectively captured the brand's edgy and youthful ethos while making the packaging a standout piece in retail spaces, enhancing brand recognition and consumer engagement in a competitive market.
