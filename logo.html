Greenbrier Holdings

Greenbrier Holdings, entrenched in agricultural tradition, required a corporate logo that honored its rich heritage while showcasing its cannabis expertise. The resulting design featured four colored bands within the letter 'G', symbolizing the four generations of farming knowledge. This design not only underscores the company's deep roots in agriculture but also highlights its commitment to quality in cannabis cultivation, manufacturing, and distribution, reinforcing its brand values and operational history.

Satori Salvation

Satori Salvation, a high-potency CBD brand, sought to encapsulate the dual qualities of its products—energizing and soothing—within its logo design. The brand aimed to emphasize its theme of enlightenment, integral to its identity. The resulting logo, luxurious in design, uses a palette of purples and blues to symbolize vitality and calmness, respectively. A woven border, evocative of the journey to enlightenment, adds a unique and sophisticated visual, aligning the logo with the brand’s luxury market positioning and embodying the essence of 'Satori'.

New England Hemp Farm

For New England Hemp Farm, a consumer CBD brand in Connecticut, the challenge was to design a logo that resonated with the brand’s heritage and location—a converted old firehouse. The stylized logo incorporates elements reflective of the firehouse architecture, creating a distinctive and memorable brand identity. This strategic design solidifies the brand's local heritage and leverages it to establish a strong, recognizable presence in the market.

Laughing Buddha

Laughing Buddha required a logo that conveyed both friendliness and modern edginess to appeal to a vibrant demographic in Washington state. The final logo design, featuring a smiling Buddha and street art influences, perfectly marries these elements. This approach not only makes the brand approachable but also infuses it with a youthful energy, distinguishing it in the competitive cannabis market.

788 Technologies

788 Technologies, a company at the intersection of science and CBD production, sought a logo that depicted the relief and joy brought by their products, along with their scientific expertise. The logo, featuring a stylized human figure encircled by simplified molecular structures, elegantly communicates the brand's focus on human-centered care enhanced by scientific innovation, supporting its identity as a technology-driven entity.

Adaptiiv Grow Technologies

Adaptiiv Grow Technologies needed a logo that mirrored the innovative and technological aspects of their cannabis cultivation systems. The design incorporates a stylized fan within the letter 'A', symbolizing their cooling technology essential for indoor cultivation. This logo not only highlights the technical sophistication of their products but also serves as a distinctive brand marker in the environmentally sustainable cultivation space.

Bison & Rose

Bison & Rose, an upscale CBD brand from Oklahoma, required a logo that intertwined local pride with a luxurious appeal. The design, featuring a bison and a rose, pays homage to Oklahoma's state symbols while presenting a refined aesthetic. This logo successfully positions the brand within the luxury segment, appealing to consumers looking for premium, locally resonant products.

Budtender Nation

Budtender Nation, envisioned as a leading social network for the budtender community, needed a logo that was both inspiring and reflective of the industry's growth. The design features a cannabis leaf rising over a horizon, symbolizing new opportunities and the burgeoning growth of the cannabis industry. This logo effectively encapsulates the brand’s mission to foster a thriving community through engagement and education.

Hannah Industries

Hannah Industries, a tier 2 cannabis producer in Washington, sought a subtle yet impactful logo that subtly nodded to cannabis without explicit imagery. The design utilizes a color scheme that hints at cannabis, packaged in a modern, minimalist style. This approach caters to a sophisticated audience, enhancing the brand's appeal by focusing on understated elegance.

Happy Camper

Happy Camper, aligned with its eco-conscious values, required a logo that evoked the essence of camping and the outdoors. The retro-inspired design uses earthy tones and vintage graphics to appeal to environmental enthusiasts and outdoor lovers, effectively communicating the brand's commitment to sustainability and its connection to national parks.

Red Dirt

Red Dirt, an Oklahoma-based cannabis brand, wanted a logo that conveyed its heritage and expertise with a vintage flair. The design incorporates a sunburst motif and gold colors, emphasizing the natural, sungrown process of their cannabis. This distinctive logo sets the brand apart, highlighting its commitment to authenticity and quality in a rustic yet sophisticated manner.
